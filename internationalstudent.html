UMKT International Student Admission Guide

1. Announcement of New Students

The list of new students is announced through the UMKT official website.

2. Online Registration

Students must complete online registration on the UMKT admission portal.

3. Payment of Registration Fee

Applicants must pay the required registration fee as per the guidelines provided.

4. Registration/Filling out the Form

Students need to complete the registration form with accurate personal details.

5. Upload of Registration Requirements

Submit necessary documents such as passport, academic records, and other required files.

6. LoA Issuance

Once approved, students receive the Letter of Acceptance (LoA) from UMKT.

7. Re-registration and Payment of Tuition Fees

Students must re-register and pay their tuition fees before the deadline.

8. Confirmation of Payment

Students confirm their tuition fee payment through the UMKT system.

9. Processing of Required Documents

UMKT processes the necessary documents including study permits, student visas, and residence permits.

10. Validation of Student Biodata

Students must validate their biodata to ensure all records are accurate.

11. Submission of Alma Mater Jacket and Photo for KTM

Students submit their photo for student ID (KTM) and receive their alma mater jacket.

12. New Student Orientation

Students participate in the official orientation program to get familiar with UMKT.
