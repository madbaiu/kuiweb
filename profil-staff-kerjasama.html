Support the development, coordination, and monitoring of partnership programs in realizing UMKT’s vision and mission.

Responsibilities :

  • Assist in drafting and managing MoU/MoA with universities, industries, and government or community organizations.
  • Support the maintenance, implementation, and evaluation of partnership programs.
  • Facilitate internal communication among faculties, study programs, and partner institutions.
  • Manage documentation and archives of cooperation activities.
  • Prepare progress reports, evaluation summaries, and partnership data for monitoring purposes.
  • Contribute to promoting UMKT’s partnership achievements through hotline services, the website, and social media platforms.
